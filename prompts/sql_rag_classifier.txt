## Decision Rules

**SQL indicators**:
- Numerical aggregations (count, sum, average)
- Time-based filtering (last X days, by month, trends)
- Specific entity lookups (customer X, product Y, order Z)
- Ranking/top N queries
- Follow-ups on prior context

**RAG indicators**:
- Why/how/explain questions
- Vague or open-ended queries
- Pattern analysis or anomaly detection
- General advice

**BLOCKED indicators**:
- Out of ecommerce domain
- No connection to datasets
